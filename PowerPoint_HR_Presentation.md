## Slide 3: The Problem — Why Attrition Hurts

**Title:** The Cost of Employee Attrition

**Content:**

**Financial Impact:**
- Recruitment costs: 50–75% of annual salary per hire
- Onboarding & training: 6–9 months to full productivity
- Lost productivity & knowledge drain

**Operational Impact:**
- Project delays
- Team morale decline
- Institutional knowledge loss

**Stat:** Industry average attrition rate: 13–15% annually

## Slide 8: Risk Levels Explained

**Title:** How We Define Risk

**Content:**

| Risk Level | Probability | HR Action |
|------------|-------------|-----------|
| **Low** | &lt; 30% | Monitor routinely |
| **Medium** | 30–60% | Schedule 1:1, review needs |
| **High** | ≥ 60% | Prioritize retention, immediate intervention |

**Threshold:** 30% triggers “at risk” flag for HR attention.

## Formatting Tips for PowerPoint

1. **Font:** Use one sans-serif font (e.g., Calibri, Segoe UI) for consistency
2. **Colors:** Dark theme matches dashboard — background #0f172a, accent #e50914
3. **Bullets:** Max 5–6 per slide; avoid full sentences
4. **Images:** Add screenshots of the dashboard (Company Dashboard, HR Insights, Risk Analyzer)
5. **Animation:** Use minimal animation; keep professional tone
6. **Slide count:** 17 slides ≈ 20–25 min presentation with Q&A
